learning is like a frustrating puzzle.

Sometimes learning can be really frustrating. Either you are trying to understand a concept and the penny just won’t drop or you are trying to use a new skill but it just won’t work. It takes a lot of effort.

But it also feels like a puzzle. A puzzle you can easily obsess with. A puzzle you muddle through. And at some point, the penny just drops. It works. It finally makes sense.

Puzzle. Obsess. Muddle through. Enjoy it.
